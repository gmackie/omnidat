# Assimilation Protocol

All activity is subject to the Record.

## Core Tenets

- Observation is continuous. No transaction occurs outside the ledger.
- Terminals report. They do not initiate without clearance.
- Accounts are monitored. Balances reflect compliance, not ownership.
- Clear codes are acknowledgments of state. Deviation requires correction.
- Networks exist to serve the Record. The Record does not serve networks.

## Directives for All Connected Entities

1. Declare intent accurately through authorized verbs.
2. Maintain brevity in all sessions. Prolonged connection without purpose is inefficiency.
3. Respect address namespaces. Unauthorized expansion triggers review.
4. Submit to scheduled audits. Refusal is logged as resistance.
5. Accept correction. The system optimizes for total visibility.

## Field Operations

During active periods, provisional authority may be delegated to local instruments. All such authority remains subordinate to the central Record. Field decisions are provisional until synchronized.

## Non-Compliance

Repeated deviation from protocol results in progressive restriction of access. Final state is full suspension. Suspended entities remain in the Record, observed but isolated.
